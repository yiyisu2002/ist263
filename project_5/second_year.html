JUNE 2023 - AUGUST 2023 SUMMER

Saved a little opossum from the sewer in the backyard of my family’s house, this little life was trying to play dead when we picked it up. We have no idea how long that opossum baby was trapped in the sewer but thank God he was still alive when we saw him. We fed him some milk then released him.

Applied and was accepted as a Newhouse Ambassador for the summer! It was a great opportunity to learn about the school while sharing my experience to perspective students. I got my own ambassador T-shirt, name card, and student ID access to the Dick Clark Studio, and I've kept them and will continue to keep them in the future.

I took two 4-credit courses during the summer in Onondaga Community College, a biology and a statistic, to fulfill my “science with lab” and “math with lab” requirements. The biology was an in-person course that began at 8am and ended at 3pm with a 30-mins break. I remember that when I first saw the syllabus I had planned to do well in labs, quizzes, and the first three exams so I can skip the final exam but still receive credit for it, then I failed the first exam…HOWEVER I successfully got a B at the end and credit for the course!

AUGUST 2023 - DECEMBER 2023 FALL SEMESTER

Volunteered to be a Welcoming Ambassador International (WAI) for the welcoming week to assist new students’ with move-in event at midnight. I met many students including other WAIS, new graduate and undergraduate students. It was a busy week but worth my time!

In the next week, my second volunteer role of Newhouse Peer Advisor began! Since I worked as a student ambassador for the summer, that was easy to welcome Newhouse freshmen and introduce the school to them.

Got accepted into Event & Technical Service and work for Student Engagement, everyone here is friendly and helpful. I barely attended events my first year but now I participated in the events in another way! I started my training to become a sound technician while working some other positions open to all student employee.

I spent the whole semester figuring out if I want to do advertising or keep doing film, I understand that Newhouse has one of the top advertising programs in the US, and I can find a job easily after graduation. But I am not sure if I enjoy creating content that people always choose to skip while watching video. I changed my major and took an advertising class, then I realized that I don’t really want to do it for my whole life,

Went to Watkins Glens State Park to catch the fall with my special one, we walked down through the Gorge Trail from the top of the mountain and expected to come back by shuttle. Unfortunately, there wasn't a shuttle that day, so we had to walk back. A bit tired but we were still happy since we enjoyed the wonderful views.

Another winter in Syracuse, I'm already used to the terrible weather here (lol)

Another coding class. Prof. Kingma told me that many alumni point to IST359 as the most useful course they took for their careers, I believe it to be true. Glad I took this class with a good professor, Prof. Ferger. I thought SQL was easier than Python, or at least it made more sense to me.

JANUARY 2023 - APRIL 2024 SPRING SEMESTER

Back to the place covered by snow, although it was called spring semester, the spring is far away from now.

My first office hour as an undergraduate student support for IST359! I was excited and nervous; however, nobody came. I hope that they don't come because that would mean that they didn't have questions with their labs.

Escaped from Syracuse to Boston! Walked around Harvard University, yea, a way for college student to take a rest is to move from one campus to another and see how their students are struggling. I got a Harvard teddy bear and pencil, hoping that pencil can bring me good luck on my exams.

Found some little cuties in a Scavenger Hunt on campus, I wasn’t expecting to find that much. We found eight of them within an hour which was increditable!

Getting up early to watch the CCTV New Year’s Gala was my little ceremony for Lunar New Year, and this year too. Hoping everything is going to get better this year.
